How the math works. Net profit = resale price − purchase − liens − closing − financing − reno − holding − selling costs. Cash ROI divides profit by actual cash invested (down payment + costs). The target-offer figure back-solves the highest purchase price that still clears your target return. Comps are pulled from public web data and turned into a $/sqft estimate — a seed value, not a CMA. Confirm reno scope with contractor bids, resale value with closed sales, and the lien payoff with a title search before you commit.
